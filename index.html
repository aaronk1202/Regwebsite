The Regulars

Four-piece covers band playing big singalong indie, rock, Britpop and party tunes for pubs, weddings, private parties and live music nights.

Booking enquirySee gig datesFacebook

Proper live covers, no awkward wedding band energy.

The Regulars have been together for over 10 years, now joined by George Axon on guitar, bringing a bigger four-piece sound to every gig.

About the band

We play guitar-driven covers with energy, humour and the right songs for the room. From pub classics to indie anthems, heavier singalongs and party finishers, the set is built to keep people watching, singing and staying for another drink.

Available for pubs, private parties, weddings, festivals and local live music events across Kent and the surrounding areas.
