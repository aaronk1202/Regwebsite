About the band

The Regulars are a four-piece covers band who have been gigging together for over 10 years, playing a mix of Brit Rock, Indie Rock and well-known classics. We perform songs by artists including Oasis, Foo Fighters, Kings of Leon, The Killers, Sam Fender, Fleetwood Mac, Pulp and Kaiser Chiefs.

Available for pubs, private parties, weddings, festivals and live music events across Kent and the surrounding areas.
